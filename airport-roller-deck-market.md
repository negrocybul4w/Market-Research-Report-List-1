<p><ul><li>Belt Connected</li><li>Chain Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airport Roller Deck market consists of two main types: belt-connected and chain-connected systems. Belt-connected roller decks use flexible belts to transport baggage smoothly, offering quieter operation and lower maintenance needs. In contrast, chain-connected systems utilize durable chains for conveying heavier loads, providing robust performance ideal for high-capacity environments. Both types enhance baggage handling efficiency, streamline operations, and improve airport logistics, ensuring timely processing of passenger luggage while adapting to varying operational demands.</p></p>
