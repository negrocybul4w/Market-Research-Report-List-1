<p><p>The aqueous polyurethane resins market primarily comprises two types: aqueous polyurethane dispersions (PUDs) and aqueous polyurethane emulsions. Aqueous polyurethane dispersions are typically used for coatings, adhesives, and sealants, offering superior performance characteristics such as flexibility and durability. Aqueous polyurethane emulsions, on the other hand, are widely employed in paints, textiles, and leather applications, providing versatility and ease of application. Both types capitalize on their waterborne formulations, enabling low VOC emissions and promoting environmental sustainability in various industries.</p></p>
