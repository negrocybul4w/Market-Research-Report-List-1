<p><p>The Automatic Tag Reader market is primarily divided into RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) types. RFID systems use electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects, offering high-speed data capture and improved inventory management. In contrast, OCR technology converts images of printed or handwritten text into machine-readable data, enabling automated document processing and data entry. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in various industries, including retail, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
