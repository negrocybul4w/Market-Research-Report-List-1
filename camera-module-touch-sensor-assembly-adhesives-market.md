<p><p>The Camera Module & Touch Sensor Assembly Adhesives market encompasses two main types: UV Curable and Non-UV Curable adhesives. UV Curable adhesives are activated by ultraviolet light, allowing for rapid curing and strong bonds, making them ideal for precision applications like camera modules. In contrast, Non-UV Curable adhesives rely on environmental factors or heat for curing, offering flexibility in application where UV exposure is not feasible. Both types are essential for ensuring durability and optimal performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
