<p><strong>The Conductivity Level Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Electrode</li><li>Suspended Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductivity level switch market primarily features two types: solid electrode and suspended electrode switches. Solid electrode switches utilize direct contact with the conductive liquid, providing a reliable means for level detection in various applications. In contrast, suspended electrode switches employ electrodes that hang within the liquid without direct contact with the tank bottom, allowing for detection in challenging environments. Both types cater to different industrial needs, ensuring accurate monitoring and control of liquid levels based on conductivity.</p></p>
