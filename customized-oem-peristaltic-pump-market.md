<p><p>The Customized (OEM) Peristaltic Pump market is segmented into two primary types: Single Channel and Multi Channel pumps. Single Channel pumps are designed for precise fluid transfer in applications requiring low flow rates, making them suitable for laboratory and medical use. In contrast, Multi Channel pumps feature several parallel pathways, allowing simultaneous pumping of multiple fluids or higher flow rates, often utilized in industrial and pharmaceutical settings. Each type is tailored to meet specific operational demands and efficiency needs.</p></p>
