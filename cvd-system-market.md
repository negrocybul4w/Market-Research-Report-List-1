<p><ul><li>Normal CVD</li><li>PECVD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) system market can be categorized into three main types: Normal CVD, PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition), and Others. Normal CVD utilizes thermal energy to facilitate chemical reactions and deposit materials on substrates, typically at higher temperatures. PECVD, on the other hand, employs plasma to enhance reaction rates at lower temperatures, allowing for better deposition control and film quality. The "Others" category includes specialized techniques like MOCVD (Metal-Organic CVD) and ATMOSPHERIC CVD, catering to unique industrial applications.</p></p>
