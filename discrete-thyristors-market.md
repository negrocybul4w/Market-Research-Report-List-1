<p><strong>The Discrete Thyristors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete thyristors market is primarily segmented into unidirectional and bidirectional thyristors. Unidirectional thyristors, designed to conduct current in one direction, are commonly used in AC and DC applications like motor control and power regulation. In contrast, bidirectional thyristors can conduct current in both directions, making them suitable for applications such as phase control and alternators. The choice between these types depends on the specific voltage, current requirements, and application needs in power electronics.</p></p>
