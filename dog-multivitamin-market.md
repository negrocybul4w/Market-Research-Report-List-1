<p><p>The dog multivitamin market can be categorized into fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, such as A, D, E, and K, are stored in the body's fatty tissues and liver, making them essential for long-term health and wellness. Water-soluble vitamins, like B vitamins and vitamin C, are not stored and must be replenished regularly through diet. Both types play crucial roles in a dog's overall health, supporting immune function, bone health, and energy metabolism.</p></p>
