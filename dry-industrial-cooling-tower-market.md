<p><ul><li>Open Cooling Tower</li><li>Closed Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry industrial cooling tower market is primarily categorized into two types: open cooling towers and closed cooling towers. Open cooling towers use ambient air to cool water, facilitating heat exchange between the two, making them efficient and cost-effective for many industrial applications. In contrast, closed cooling towers contain the cooling water in a closed loop, preventing contamination and reducing water loss, thus providing enhanced efficiency and water conservation. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on operational requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
