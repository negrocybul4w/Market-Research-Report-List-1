<p><strong>The Engineered Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three Layers Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered flooring is categorized into two main types: three-layer and multilayer. Three-layer engineered flooring consists of a top veneer layer, a middle core of plywood or high-density fiberboard, and a bottom backing layer, providing stability and aesthetic appeal. Multilayer engineered flooring, on the other hand, features multiple layers that enhance durability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for various environments. Both types offer a balance of beauty and functionality, appealing to a wide range of consumers.</p></p>
