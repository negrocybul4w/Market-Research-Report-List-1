<p><p>Factory agriculture, also known as industrial agriculture, is a large-scale farming system that utilizes advanced technologies and methods to maximize production efficiency and output. Additionally, technological advancements such as precision farming, automation, and biotechnology are enhancing productivity and reducing costs in factory farming. However, concerns regarding animal welfare, environmental impact, and food safety are prompting shifts toward more sustainable practices and alternative food sources, such as plant-based proteins.
