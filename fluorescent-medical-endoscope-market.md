<p><p>The fluorescent medical endoscope market can be categorized into two primary types: manual focus and electric focus. Manual focus endoscopes require the operator to adjust the lens position manually to achieve clear imaging, offering simplicity and control during procedures. In contrast, electric focus endoscopes utilize motorized mechanisms for automatic focus adjustments, enhancing precision and ease of use. Both types serve essential roles in diagnostics and treatment, with electric options increasingly favored for their efficiency and improved visualization during medical examinations.</p></p>
