<p><p>The Ground-Based Augmentation System (GBAS) enhances the accuracy and reliability of GPS signals for aviation purposes. The market is divided into two main types: GBAS Stations and GBAS Receivers. GBAS Stations are fixed ground facilities that provide corrective signals to improve GPS accuracy for aircraft in the vicinity, facilitating safe landings and operations. GBAS Receivers are onboard aircraft systems that process these enhanced signals, ensuring precise navigation and approach capabilities, ultimately leading to improved flight safety and efficiency.</p></p>
