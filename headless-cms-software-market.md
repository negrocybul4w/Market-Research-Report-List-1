<p><p>Headless CMS software can be categorized into two main market types: API-driven and Git-based systems. API-driven headless CMSs utilize a robust API to deliver content to various front-end applications, ensuring flexibility and scalability for developers. In contrast, Git-based headless CMSs integrate with version control systems, allowing content to be managed as code, enhancing collaboration among development teams. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to different use cases and preferences in content management and delivery.</p></p>
