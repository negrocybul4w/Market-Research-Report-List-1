<p><p>The Household Non-invasive Ventilator market comprises three main types: CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure), BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure), and APAP (Automatic Positive Airway Pressure). CPAP delivers a constant airflow to keep airways open, ideal for obstructive sleep apnea. BiPAP offers two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, supporting patients with more complex respiratory issues. APAP adjusts pressure automatically based on detected breathing patterns, providing personalized therapy. Together, these devices enhance patient comfort and compliance in at-home respiratory care.</p></p>
