<p><p>The hydrogen peroxide sterilization equipment market comprises two main types: vaporized hydrogen peroxide sterilization equipment and fogging hydrogen peroxide sterilization equipment. Vaporized hydrogen peroxide systems utilize a vaporization process to distribute hydrogen peroxide evenly in enclosed spaces, effectively disinfecting surfaces and air. In contrast, fogging equipment creates a fine mist of hydrogen peroxide, allowing for rapid and thorough sterilization in larger areas. Both methods are widely used in healthcare and industrial settings to ensure effective microbial control.</p></p>
