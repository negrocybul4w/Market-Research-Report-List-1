<p><p>The large equipment seats market primarily consists of two types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks and vibrations, providing a stable and comfortable ride for operators. In contrast, air suspension seats utilize an air-filled bladder system to adjust to the operator's weight and movements, offering superior comfort and support. Both types are essential in enhancing operator efficiency and reducing fatigue in various heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
