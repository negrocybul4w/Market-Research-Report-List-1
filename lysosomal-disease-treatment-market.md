<p><p>The lysosomal disease treatment market includes several types of therapies. Hematopoietic stem cell transplantation involves replacing defective blood system cells to restore enzyme function. Chaperone therapies stabilize mutant enzymes, enhancing their function and promoting proper cellular activity. Together, these approaches aim to address the underlying causes of lysosomal storage disorders, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
