<p><strong>The Marine Engine Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Monitoring</li><li>Remote Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Engine Monitoring System market is divided into two main types: On-site Monitoring and Remote Monitoring. On-site Monitoring involves direct, real-time assessment of engine performance and condition using sensors and display systems located on the vessel, allowing crew members to make immediate decisions. In contrast, Remote Monitoring enables continuous tracking of engine parameters via satellite or internet, allowing operators to oversee multiple vessels from a distance. This enhances operational efficiency, reduces downtime, and improves maintenance scheduling.</p></p>
