<p><strong>The Mechanical Tappets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical tappets market comprises two primary types: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets are simpler and generally more cost-effective, but they experience higher friction and wear, making them suitable for older engine designs. Roller tappets, on the other hand, utilize a roller at the follower's end to reduce friction and enhance efficiency, providing better performance and longevity, particularly in high-performance engines. The choice between them often depends on the application, performance requirements, and maintenance considerations.</p></p>
