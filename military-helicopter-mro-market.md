Airframe Heavy Maintenance focuses on the structural integrity and repair of the helicopter's body, ensuring it meets operational safety standards. Engine Maintenance involves the servicing and refurbishment of the helicopter's engines to maintain performance and reliability. Component Maintenance addresses the repair or replacement of specific parts such as avionics, rotors, and hydraulic systems, ensuring overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
