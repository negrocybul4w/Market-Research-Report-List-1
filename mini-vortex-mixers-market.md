<p><p>Mini vortex mixers are compact laboratory devices designed for quickly mixing small volumes of liquids. In the market, two notable types are touch mode vortex mixers and infrared (IR) infrared vortex mixers. Touch mode vortex mixers operate by activating the mixing function when pressure is applied, ensuring ease of use and control. In contrast, IR vortex mixers utilize infrared technology for hands-free operation, detecting sample presence and adjusting mixing intensity automatically, enhancing efficiency and precision in laboratory settings.</p></p>
