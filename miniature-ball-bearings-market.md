<p><ul><li>Open Miniature Ball Bearings</li><li>Dust Over Miniature Ball Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature ball bearings market features two primary types: open miniature ball bearings and dust cover miniature ball bearings. Open miniature ball bearings are designed without shields, allowing for easy lubrication and reduced friction, making them ideal for applications with minimal contamination. In contrast, dust cover miniature ball bearings are equipped with protective shields to block dirt and debris, ensuring prolonged service life in harsher environments. Both types cater to various industries, emphasizing performance and durability.</p></p>
