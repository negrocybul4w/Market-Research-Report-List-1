<p><p>NBR foam products, primarily utilized in various industries for insulation and cushioning, are categorized into two main types: closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber. Closed cell rubber features tightly packed cells that limit moisture absorption, making it ideal for applications requiring high insulation and resistance to environmental factors. Semi-closed cell rubber, on the other hand, has a slightly more open structure, allowing for better airflow while still offering some insulation. Both types serve distinct applications across automotive, construction, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
