<p><p>The non-tyrosine kinase inhibitors market encompasses several key categories, including mTOR inhibitors, RAF/MEK inhibitors, and CDK inhibitors. mTOR inhibitors target the mechanistic target of rapamycin pathway, crucial for cell growth and proliferation, thus impacting cancer therapy. RAF/MEK inhibitors focus on disrupting the RAF/MEK/ERK signaling pathway, which is often activated in cancers. CDK inhibitors interfere with cyclin-dependent kinases, regulating the cell cycle, and are vital in treating various malignancies. These inhibitors offer targeted approaches in cancer treatment.</p></p>
