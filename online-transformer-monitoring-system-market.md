<p><p>The Online Transformer Monitoring System market encompasses various types, including Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA), Partial Discharge (PD), Bushing Monitoring (BM), Transformer Fire Protection (TFC), and others. DGA assesses gas levels in transformer oil to detect insulation breakdown. PD monitoring identifies insulation defects through electrical discharge detection. BM focuses on monitoring transformer bushing conditions for reliability. TFC emphasizes fire protection systems to enhance safety. Together, these technologies ensure efficient operation and maintenance of transformers in electrical systems.</p></p>
