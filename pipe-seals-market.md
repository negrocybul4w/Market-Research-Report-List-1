<p><p>The Pipe Seals market comprises three primary types: gaskets, O-rings, and other seals. Gaskets are used to create a tight seal between two surfaces, preventing leaks in pipelines and machinery. O-rings are circular seals that fit into groove applications, ensuring a reliable barrier against fluid or gas leakage. Other seals encompass a variety of sealing solutions like compression seals and flange seals, tailored for specific industrial needs. Together, these components play a crucial role in maintaining system integrity and efficiency.</p></p>
