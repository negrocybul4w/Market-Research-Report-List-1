<p><strong>The Polyurethane Tension Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversal Tension</li><li>Longitudinal Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane tension screen market can be categorized into two main types: transversal tension and longitudinal tension screens. Transversal tension screens are designed to accommodate the screen's width, providing consistent material flow and increased durability. In contrast, longitudinal tension screens are oriented along the length of the screen, enhancing performance in applications requiring linear material movement. Both types are utilized in various industries for their high flexibility, resistance to wear, and ability to efficiently separate materials in screening processes.</p></p>
