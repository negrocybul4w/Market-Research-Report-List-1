<p><ul><li>Convection Reflow Oven</li><li>Vapour Phase Reflow Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflow ovens are essential tools in the PCB and semiconductor markets, facilitating the soldering process by melting solder paste to create reliable electrical connections. Convection reflow ovens utilize hot air to evenly distribute heat, ensuring consistent soldering across various components. In contrast, vapor phase reflow ovens employ vaporized solvents to provide precise temperature control and uniform heating, minimizing thermal stress on substrates. Both types enhance operational efficiency and product quality, catering to diverse manufacturing requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
