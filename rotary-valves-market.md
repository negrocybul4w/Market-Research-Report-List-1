Electric motor-driven rotary valves utilize electrical energy for precise and efficient control of fluid flow, commonly found in applications requiring high-speed operation. In contrast, hydraulic motor-driven rotary valves leverage pressurized fluid to generate rotational movement, suitable for heavy-duty applications that demand high torque and power. Each type caters to specific operational needs, enhancing performance across diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
