<p><p>The Sea Water Nasal Spray market is primarily categorized into two types: isotonic and hypertonic solutions. Isotonic sea water nasal sprays contain a saline concentration similar to that of the body's cells, helping to moisturize and relieve nasal congestion without causing irritation. In contrast, hypertonic sea water nasal sprays have a higher saline concentration, which can promote mucus drainage and reduce swelling in the nasal passages. Both types are used for treating symptoms of nasal congestion, allergies, and sinus issues.</p></p>
