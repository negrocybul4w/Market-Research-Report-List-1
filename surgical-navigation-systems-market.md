<p><p>Surgical navigation systems encompass various technologies that assist surgeons in accurately locating surgical instruments and planning operations. Optical surgical navigation systems use cameras and reflective markers to track instruments based on visual data. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic fields to determine the position of tools in relation to the patient's anatomy. Other market types may include hybrid systems that incorporate both optical and electromagnetic technologies or advanced software solutions that enhance navigational accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
