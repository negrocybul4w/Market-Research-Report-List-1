<p><p>Thermal Magnetic Circuit Breakers are essential protective devices that combine thermal and magnetic mechanisms to safeguard electrical circuits from overloads and short circuits. The thermal component responds to prolonged overloads by utilizing a bimetallic strip that bends with heat, whereas the magnetic component provides instantaneous protection by reacting to sudden surges in current. This dual-functionality makes thermal magnetic circuit breakers highly versatile and reliable for various applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p><p>The Thermal Magnetic Circuit Breakers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
