<p><p>The viral detection market includes several key types: Plaque Assays, PCR, ELISA, and others. Plaque assays measure viral infectivity by observing the formation of plaques in cell cultures. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies viral genetic material for precise identification and quantification. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects viral antigens or antibodies, providing insight into exposure or infection status. Other methods may include rapid antigen tests, next-generation sequencing, and serological tests, enhancing the overall capabilities for viral detection and diagnostics.</p></p>
