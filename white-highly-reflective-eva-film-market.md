<p><p>The White Highly Reflective EVA Film market primarily includes two types: Normal EVA and Anti-PID EVA. Normal EVA is used for standard applications where basic encapsulation and reflectivity are needed, enhancing solar panel efficiency. In contrast, Anti-PID EVA incorporates advanced features to prevent Potential Induced Degradation (PID), ensuring longer-lasting performance and reliability of solar panels. Both types serve specific needs within the solar industry, focusing on durability, efficiency, and performance enhancement in photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
