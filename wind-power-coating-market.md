Ceramic coatings provide excellent thermal stability and abrasion resistance, protecting surfaces from harsh environmental conditions. Metal coatings, often used for their durability and protective qualities, help minimize wear and tear on turbine parts. Together, these coatings play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and lifespan of wind energy infrastructure.</p></p>
